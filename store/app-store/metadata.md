## Description

Groove Metronome helps musicians see, hear, and trust the click.

Built by Nathaniel School of Music, it combines a clean practice metronome with visual beat maps, accent control, subdivisions, polyrhythm, polymeter, rhythm assist tools, setlists, and audio/MIDI analysis.

Use it for daily practice, teaching, rehearsals, and stage work. The main wheel stays readable on phone, iPad, and desktop. Setlist Studio gives performers a concert-focused view with big controls, song timing, quick tempo changes, sharing, backup, and panic recovery. Analyzer helps you import audio or MIDI, estimate tempo, inspect markers, and then apply the tempo only when you choose.

Highlights:
- Large visual metronome wheel
- Time signatures, subdivisions, accents, and mute states
- Per-beat beat map patterns
- Polyrhythm and polymeter practice views
- Jazz, dotted, and triplet rhythm assist
- Setlist Studio for concerts and rehearsals
- Audio and MIDI tempo analysis
- Offline-ready browser core
- Tasteful sampled click sounds
- iPhone and iPad friendly controls

Groove Metronome is designed to feel simple first, then deep when you need it.

## Review Notes

Groove Metronome 1.0 is free and does not require an account.

The app is a musician utility. Core flows to review:
1. Open the Metronome tab and start/stop the click.
2. Adjust BPM, time signature, subdivisions, accents, and sound.
3. Switch between Beat Map, Levels, Polyrhythm, and Polymeter.
4. Open Analyzer, import local audio or MIDI, and inspect tempo/markers.
5. Open Setlist Studio and start a stage/concert view.

Microphone access is requested only when the user chooses live tempo detection. Imported audio/MIDI files and live recordings are processed locally in the app for the current release.
